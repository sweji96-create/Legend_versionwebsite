Supplier & Partner Onboarding

Nashco Global welcomes partnerships with qualified suppliers and vendors across the GCC region. We seek reliable partners who share our commitment to quality, compliance, and customer service.

Partnership Opportunities

  • Refrigerants & Chemicals: Suppliers of industrial-grade refrigerants and chemical products
  • Automotive Parts: OEM and aftermarket automotive component suppliers
  • Manufacturing: Raw materials, components, and equipment suppliers
  • Trading: Cross-border trading partners and logistics providers

Supplier Requirements

To become a Nashco Global supplier, you must meet the following criteria:

  • Valid business registration and trade licenses
  • Quality certifications (ISO 9001 or equivalent preferred)
  • Compliance with GCC regulatory standards (ESMA, SASO, etc.)
  • Product certifications and test reports
  • Competitive pricing and reliable delivery schedules
  • Full product documentation (SDS, certificates of analysis, etc.)

Onboarding Process

  1. Initial Inquiry: Submit your company profile and product/service information
  2. Evaluation: Our procurement team reviews your submission
  3. Documentation: Provide required certifications and compliance documents
  4. Sample Testing: Product samples evaluated by our quality team (if applicable)
  5. Agreement: Negotiate terms and sign supplier agreement
  6. Onboarding: Complete registration in our supplier management system
