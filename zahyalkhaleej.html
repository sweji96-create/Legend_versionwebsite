About Zahy Al Khaleej

Zahy Al Khaleej, a flagship division of Nashco Global, specializes in providing high-quality chemical solutions to diverse industries across the GCC region. With state-of-the-art facilities and a commitment to excellence, we deliver products that meet international standards of quality, safety, and reliability.

Our comprehensive range of chemical products serves sectors including manufacturing, construction, automotive, agriculture, and more. We pride ourselves on our technical expertise, customer-centric approach, and unwavering dedication to safety and environmental responsibility.
