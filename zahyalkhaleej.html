About Zahy Al Khaleej Group

Zahy Al Khaleej Group is a prestigious conglomerate with a diversified portfolio spanning multiple sectors across the GCC region. Known for its commitment to excellence, innovation, and sustainable growth, the Group has established itself as a leader in regional business development and industrial advancement.

Nashco Global operates proudly under the Zahy Al Khaleej umbrella, benefiting from the Group's extensive resources, regional expertise, and strategic vision. This affiliation enables Nashco Global to deliver world-class solutions across chemicals, automotive, manufacturing, and trading sectors with unparalleled support and capabilities.
