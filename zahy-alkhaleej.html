Zahy Alkhaleej LLC, Oman

A Nashco Group Company – Excellence in Refrigerants, Chemicals, Automotive, and Trading

Company Overview

Zahy Alkhaleej LLC, a Nashco Group company headquartered in Sohar, Oman, provides wholesale, import, and export services for refrigerant gases, industrial chemicals, automotive spare parts, and vehicle trading. The company serves contractors, OEMs, distributors, and commercial clients across Oman and the GCC, emphasizing regulatory compliance, safe handling, and reliable logistics.

Industry Focus

Refrigerant Gases & Chemicals

Supply of certified refrigerants and industrial chemicals with full MSDS and regulatory documentation.

  • Common refrigerants: R134a, R22, R404A, R407C, R410A, R290
  • Packaging: cylinders, drums, and bulk deliveries
  • Compliance: Montreal Protocol alignment; local GCC regulations

Refrigeration Systems

Support for commercial and industrial refrigeration, including spare parts and consumables.

Automotive & Aftermarket

Distribution of OEM and aftermarket components, diagnostics equipment, and workshop supplies.

  • Parts for light and heavy vehicles
  • After-sales service support and technical documentation

Vehicle Trading

Inspected import/export of new and used vehicles with logistics and customs support across the GCC.

Primary Focus

Zahy Alkhaleej’s core priorities are:

  • Regulatory Compliance & Safety: Full adherence to Montreal Protocol provisions and regional safety standards.
  • Supply Chain Reliability: Robust warehousing in Sohar and Muscat; coordinated cross-border logistics.
  • Customer Support: Technical documentation, MSDS availability, and after-sales service.
  • OEM & Trade Partnerships: Long-term relationships with manufacturers and regional dealers.
  • Sustainability Transition: Promoting low-GWP alternatives and responsible recycling.
