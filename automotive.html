Automotive Division

Leading Automotive Solutions Across the GCC

Automotive Excellence

Nashco Global's Automotive Division delivers comprehensive automotive solutions through two specialized business units: Nashco Max Car FZE for car trading and Zahy Auto Spare for spare parts trading. Together, these divisions provide end-to-end automotive services to businesses and consumers across the GCC region.

Our Business Units

Nashco Max Car FZE

Car Trading Division

Nashco Max Car FZE is our premier car trading division, offering a comprehensive range of new and pre-owned vehicles from leading global manufacturers. Based in a free zone, we provide efficient import-export services and competitive pricing for individual and corporate clients.

Key Services:

  • New and certified pre-owned vehicles
  • Fleet solutions for corporate clients
  • Import and export facilitation
  • Vehicle financing assistance
  • After-sales service coordination
  • Comprehensive vehicle inspection and certification

Zahy Auto Spare

Spare Parts Trading Division

Zahy Auto Spare specializes in the trading of genuine and aftermarket automotive spare parts. With an extensive inventory and strong relationships with manufacturers and distributors, we ensure prompt delivery of high-quality parts for all vehicle makes and models.

Key Services:

  • Genuine OEM parts from authorized distributors
  • High-quality aftermarket parts
  • Bulk ordering for workshops and dealers
  • Fast delivery and logistics support
  • Technical consultation and parts identification
  • Warranty and quality assurance
