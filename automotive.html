Our Automotive Division

Nashco Global's Automotive Division represents our commitment to providing comprehensive automotive solutions across the GCC region. Through our two specialized brands - Nashco Max Car FZE and Zahy Auto Spare - we deliver premium vehicles and quality spare parts that meet the diverse needs of our customers.

Our Automotive Brands

Nashco Max Car FZE

Our car trading division specializes in importing and distributing premium vehicles to the GCC market. We offer a carefully curated selection of new and pre-owned vehicles from world-renowned manufacturers, ensuring quality, reliability, and value for money. Our expert team provides comprehensive services including vehicle sourcing, inspection, documentation, and after-sales support.

  • Premium & Luxury Vehicles
