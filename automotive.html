About Our Automotive Division

Nashco Global's Automotive Division comprises two specialized entities delivering comprehensive automotive solutions across the GCC region. Through Nashco Max Car FZE and Zahy Auto Spare, we provide premium car trading services and high-quality spare parts to meet diverse automotive needs.

Our Automotive Services

Nashco Max Car FZE

Premier car trading division offering a wide range of new and pre-owned vehicles. We specialize in sourcing, importing, and distributing quality automobiles with comprehensive after-sales support and warranty services.

Zahy Auto Spare

Comprehensive spare parts trading division providing genuine and aftermarket automotive parts for all major vehicle brands. Our extensive inventory ensures quick availability of parts with guaranteed quality and competitive pricing.

Quality Assurance

All vehicles and parts undergo rigorous quality checks. We maintain partnerships with authorized dealers and suppliers to ensure authenticity, reliability, and customer satisfaction in every transaction.

Customer Support

Dedicated customer service team providing expert consultation, technical support, and after-sales services. We're committed to building long-term relationships through exceptional service and support.
