About Us

Nashco Global: Diversified Excellence Since 1994

Since 1994, Nashco Global has been a trusted name across the GCC, delivering solutions in Chemicals, Automotive, Manufacturing, and Trading. Headquartered in Muscat, Oman, with strong professional ties to Kerala and the United Arab Emirates, we have grown into a multi‑domain enterprise recognized for innovation, operational excellence, and compliance.

Our journey began with a vision to empower industries and communities by building systems that are robust, scalable, and future‑ready. Today, Nashco Global serves clients in more than 50 countries, supported by a leadership team that combines strategic foresight with practical execution.

Chairman

Over three decades, he has transformed Nashco Global into a diversified enterprise serving thousands of clients worldwide. His leadership emphasizes sustainable growth, ethical practices, and a relentless pursuit of excellence, ensuring the company remains aligned with GCC regulations and international standards.
