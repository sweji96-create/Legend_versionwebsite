About Nashco Global

Excellence in Industrial Solutions Since 1994

Our Story

Established in 1994, Nashco Global has grown from a regional enterprise to a diversified industrial powerhouse serving the GCC and beyond. Our journey of over three decades represents unwavering commitment to excellence, innovation, and sustainable growth.

With operations spanning across chemicals, automotive, manufacturing, and trading sectors, we have built a reputation for delivering world-class products and services that meet the evolving needs of our clients.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative, sustainable solutions across our diversified business sectors while maintaining the highest standards of quality, safety, and customer satisfaction. We strive to be the preferred partner for industries seeking reliability and excellence.

Our Vision

To be the leading force in empowering industries and communities across the GCC and beyond, driving innovation, sustainability, and excellence in everything we do. We envision a future where Nashco Global continues to set benchmarks in industrial excellence.
