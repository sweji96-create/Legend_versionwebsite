About Nashco Global

Three Decades of Excellence, Innovation, and Growth

Our Story

Founded in 1994, Nashco Global began with a simple yet powerful vision: to become the most trusted and innovative industrial conglomerate in the GCC region. What started as a focused enterprise has evolved into a diversified group of companies serving multiple industries across the Middle East and beyond.

Through three decades of operation, we have consistently demonstrated our ability to adapt, innovate, and excel. Our growth has been driven by a commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and a deep understanding of the regional market dynamics.

Today, Nashco Global stands as a testament to the power of vision, perseverance, and strategic thinking. We operate multiple business divisions, each a leader in its respective field, united by our core values and commitment to excellence.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional value to our customers, stakeholders, and communities by providing innovative, high-quality products and services across our diverse business portfolio. We strive to be the preferred partner for businesses seeking reliability, excellence, and sustainable growth in the GCC region.

Our Vision

To be the leading diversified industrial conglomerate in the GCC, recognized for our innovation, integrity, and positive impact on the industries and communities we serve. We envision a future where Nashco Global continues to set benchmarks for excellence while contributing to the economic development and prosperity of the region.
