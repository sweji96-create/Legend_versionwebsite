About Nashco Global

30+ Years of Industrial Excellence

Our Story

Established in 1994

Nashco Global was founded with a vision to become the GCC region's most trusted industrial solutions provider. From our modest beginnings, we have grown into a diversified conglomerate with operations spanning chemicals, automotive, manufacturing, and international trading.

Over three decades, we have built a reputation for excellence, reliability, and innovation. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has been the cornerstone of our success, enabling us to forge lasting partnerships with leading businesses across the Middle East and beyond.

Our Mission

To deliver world-class industrial solutions that drive growth, innovation, and sustainability across the Middle East. We strive to be our clients' most trusted partner, providing excellence in chemicals, automotive, manufacturing, and trading services while maintaining the highest standards of quality, integrity, and customer service.

Our Vision

To be the leading diversified industrial conglomerate in the GCC region, recognized for our innovation, quality, and commitment to sustainable growth. We envision a future where Nashco Global sets the benchmark for industrial excellence, creating value for our stakeholders while contributing to the economic development of the region.
