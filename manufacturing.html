Precision Engineering & Quality Production in Riyadh

Nashco Plastic Manufacturing Factory, Riyadh

Located in the heart of Saudi Arabia's capital, our state-of-the-art plastic manufacturing facility represents Nashco Global's commitment to industrial excellence. Equipped with cutting-edge machinery and operated by highly skilled professionals, we produce a wide range of high-quality plastic products that serve diverse industrial and commercial applications.

Our Manufacturing Capabilities

Advanced Production Lines

Our factory features multiple production lines equipped with the latest technology in plastic manufacturing, including injection molding, extrusion, and blow molding capabilities. We maintain strict quality control at every stage of production.

Custom Manufacturing

We specialize in custom plastic products tailored to specific client requirements. Our engineering team works closely with customers to develop optimal solutions, from initial design to final production.

Quality Assurance

All our products undergo rigorous testing and quality checks to ensure they meet international standards. We are ISO certified and committed to delivering products that exceed customer expectations.

Sustainable Practices

Environmental responsibility is at the core of our operations. We use eco-friendly materials where possible, implement recycling programs, and continuously work to minimize our environmental footprint.
