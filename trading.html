Our Trading Operations

Nashco Global's Trading Division operates through two strategic entities, connecting markets and facilitating international commerce across the GCC and beyond.

Nashco General Trading LLC, Ajman

Based in Ajman, UAE, our general trading division handles diverse product categories including industrial equipment, consumer goods, building materials, and specialized products. We leverage strong supplier relationships and efficient logistics to deliver competitive solutions across regional markets.

Nashco International Trade Links, India

Our Indian operations facilitate bilateral trade between the Indian subcontinent and GCC markets. We specialize in sourcing quality products from Indian manufacturers and exporters, managing end-to-end logistics, customs clearance, and delivery across Gulf countries.

Trade Partnerships

We build lasting partnerships with manufacturers, suppliers, and distributors globally. Our extensive network enables us to source products efficiently, negotiate favorable terms, and provide clients with access to diverse international markets.

Logistics & Supply Chain

Comprehensive logistics solutions including freight forwarding, warehousing, customs clearance, and distribution. We ensure timely delivery and cost-effective supply chain management for all trading operations.
