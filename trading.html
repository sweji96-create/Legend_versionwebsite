Trading Division

Global Trading Networks Connecting Markets

International Trading Excellence

Nashco Global's Trading Division operates through two strategic entities: Nashco General Trading LLC in Ajman, UAE, and Nashco International Trade Links in India. Together, they form a powerful network facilitating international commerce across continents, connecting suppliers and buyers with efficient logistics and deep market knowledge.

Our Trading Entities

Nashco General Trading LLC, Ajman

United Arab Emirates

Based in Ajman Free Zone, our UAE trading entity serves as a strategic hub for import-export operations across the GCC and Middle East region. We handle diverse product categories including industrial equipment, consumer goods, construction materials, and specialized commodities.

  • Import and export facilitation
  • Customs clearance and documentation
  • Warehousing and distribution
  • Product sourcing and procurement

Nashco International Trade Links, India

India

Our Indian operations provide access to one of the world's largest and most dynamic markets. With deep local knowledge and extensive supplier networks, we facilitate trade between India and the GCC, offering competitive pricing and reliable supply chains for a wide range of products and commodities.

  • India-GCC trade facilitation
  • Supplier identification and verification
  • Quality control and inspection
  • Logistics and shipping coordination
