Nashco Global - A Legacy of Excellence

Since 1994, Nashco Global has been at the forefront of industrial innovation and excellence in the GCC region. We are a diversified conglomerate with a proven track record of delivering world-class solutions across multiple industries, including chemicals, automotive, manufacturing, and international trading.

Operating under the prestigious Zahy Al Khaleej Group, Nashco Global has established itself as a trusted partner for businesses seeking excellence, reliability, and innovation. Our commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and sustainable growth has made us a leader in the regional industrial landscape.
