About Us

Our Legacy

Since 1994, Nashco Global has been at the forefront of industrial innovation in the GCC region. As a proud member of the Zahy al Khaleej Group, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for businesses seeking excellence in chemicals, automotive solutions, manufacturing, and international trading.

Our Mission

To deliver world-class industrial solutions that drive growth, innovation, and sustainability across the Middle East. We are committed to excellence, integrity, and building lasting partnerships with our clients.

  • Innovation and continuous improvement
  • Customer-centric approach
  • Sustainability and responsibility
